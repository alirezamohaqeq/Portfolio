ALEE

I’m ALEE, a filmmaker and motion designer with a background in literature. I explore the intersection of narrative film and digital media.

I work with brands, agencies and artists to create cinematic visuals for campaigns, social media and identity work, producing end-to-end video content, including scripting, filming, editing, and post-production. I’m fluent in English and enjoy collaborating with teams across different cultures and markets.
